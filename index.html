Francis Ewe’s Publishing House and Gallery

100 Seel Street is a new creative space within the heart of Liverpool’s Georgian quarter. The derelict grade-two-listed building had been uninhabited for over fifty years and is currently in the process of being reanimated as the home of Francis Ewe’s Publishing House & Gallery.

Our long and rich history of collaborative projects within the city has contributed hugely to the grassroots artistic commutiny, and to the broader city-centre redevelopment. You’re welcome to make an appointment to view our exhibition spaces across three floors, suitable for workshops, filming, private group events, and to showcase your work.

Please direct all enquiries to info@fewe.uk
